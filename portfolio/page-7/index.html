The Patchworks Framework

A community-driven design process grounded in care, humility, and iteration.

embed first
Designers begin by showing up—volunteering, listening, asking questions. Not to diagnose, but to understand how support systems already operate.

the intentional meander
Rather than follow a linear model, the process meanders. Insights surface slowly, shaped by trust, time, and lived expertise.

prototype lightly
Most ideas never leave paper. The ones that do are sketched, Photoshopped, or mocked up in cardboard. Iteration happens at low fidelity to lower stakes and invite honest feedback from stakeholders and community partners.

refine as if it's final
Because there may only be one shot at implementation, every detail is carefully worked out. The first prototype is often the last.

tools stay, and grow
Interventions are documented and handed off—Creative Commons prompts, visual guides, and implementation notes. The goal is sustainability without dependency.

communicate clearly
A quilted systems map helps others see the work at a glance—where the patches go, and why they matter.
