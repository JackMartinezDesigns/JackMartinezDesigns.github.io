Illustrations and Publications

Illustration and graphic design are at the center of my process, whether or not they become the features of final designs.

My process is very analog, using traditional craft and production techniques to make originals and print productions. In a world of memes and AI, this hand-made approach allows me to create aesthetics that stand out while at the same time feel honest.

This letterpress print edition was set with hand-carved linoleum base, and a letterpress frame complete with custom laser-engraved phoenetic characters.

This limited print of postcards started with crayon and craft paper, using accessible craft materials to celebrate teh impact of the US Post Office and drive a vote-by-mai campaign.

Other prints worked with local arts organizations to promote Local business development in NC.
