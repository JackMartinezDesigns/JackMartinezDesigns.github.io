"Choose Grace" is a speculative futures video intended to draw on the tension between empathy and self-interest. It deals with concepts of domestic labor, matriarchal roles in the home, and the demands of an equitable relationship. I know it is provocative, but please tear it to shreds in the comments, the goal is to start the conversation about these themes.

A materials exploration revealed a way to demonstrate this issue. The tap-tap-tapping in class helps the tappers focus, but distracts everyone else, prioritizing self interest over empathy. The artifacts are made out of a resin-cast impressions of pencil tapping display compromising roughly-fit solutions to these social issues.
