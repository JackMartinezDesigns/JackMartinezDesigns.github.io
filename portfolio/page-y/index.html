White Label Services

Custom design and production, made with care.

I offer white label design and fabrication services for small businesses, organizations, and creative collaborators looking to bring custom products to life—without the overhead of managing in-house production.

Whether you're a café dreaming of branded mugs that customers actually want to take home, a bike shop in need of custom merch that speaks to your community, or a corporate team looking to elevate your workspaces with thoughtful desk tools, I can help make it happen.

Services include:

  • Small-batch production runs of custom-designed goods
  • Ceramic coffee mugs, designed, prototyped, and produced for your brand
  • Soft goods and accessories for bike shops and outdoor communities
  • Custom deal toys used to celebrate big business deals, custom toys designed to reflect your business which are offered as gits to new partners
  • Packaging and brand integration might include custom stamps of your brand's logo for a seamless customer experience
  • Poster Design for community and public events

All projects are designed collaboratively, with an emphasis on quality, local sourcing when possible, and low-waste production methods.

Freelance Design Services

Design support for startups, inventors, and small businesses.

Have an idea, but need help bringing it to life? I offer flexible freelance design services to support your team at any stage of the process—from napkin sketch to production-ready files.

Whether you’re refining a prototype, polishing technical drawings, or exploring how to make your concept manufacturable, I bring both creative problem-solving and practical fabrication experience to the table.

Freelance services include:

  • Concept development and early-stage ideation
  • Prototyping support using a wide range of materials and processes
  • Technical drawing and schematic refinement for production and sourcing
  • Design-for-manufacturing consultations tailored to your scale and budget
  • Material and sourcing research for sustainable or small-batch runs

No two projects are alike. I work closely with clients to understand your goals and figure out what kind of design support makes the most sense for your workflow.
