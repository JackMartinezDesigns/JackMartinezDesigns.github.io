Wild Persimmon™ Bike Bags

The idea for this bike bag line began in a business class, where I learned that strong products emerge from a clear market insight paired with real access to that market. As I finished graduate school, I realized I was deeply embedded in cycling communities—through friends on university teams, relationships with local bike shops in Providence and Raleigh, and my own daily riding—which gave me direct feedback and reach.

My insight was simple: serious riders rely on small, utilitarian saddle bags, yet these essential items almost exclusively came in muted colors like black, brown, and olive, despite a clear appetite for more expressive design.

I began prototyping by making bold, intentionally impractical versions for myself—using bright colors and unconventional materials—which immediately validated demand when fellow cyclists repeatedly asked where they could buy one.

Refining the designs into durable, weather-ready products, I partnered with a local bike shop to grow the concept into a small line of bike accessories, now sold under my private label through Wild Persimmon Studio.
